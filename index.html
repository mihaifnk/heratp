Herat App

Welcome to Herat - Hospitals and Emergency Rooms Availability Tracking App

Our app is designed to help you see the number of patients waiting in queues at various emergency rooms. By providing this information, the app aims to assist you in making informed decisions about where to seek immediate medical care.

About Our App

The Herat is a crowd-sourced platform where users can both view and update the waiting times at emergency rooms. This community-driven approach ensures that the data is as current and accurate as possible, relying on the contributions of users like you.

Key Features

  • View waiting times at various emergency rooms, updated by users.
  • Contribute by updating the queue numbers, but only if you are in the vicinity of the hospital.
  • Hospitals without updates in the last 24 hours are highlighted differently for informed decisions.
  • Time-stamped updates allow you to know how recent the information is.
  • Designed to be a helpful tool for non-critical decisions. Always call emergency services in critical situations.
  • No limits on the number of updates you can provide, fostering a dynamic and up-to-date platform.
